About Nicole

A clinically attentive practice for adults in Illinois.

Nicole Glass is a Licensed Clinical Professional Counselor providing treatment for adults in Illinois. Her work began within a psychodynamic framework and has evolved through psychoanalytic study and clinical practice.

She works with anxiety, depression, and difficulties related to self-esteem and self-worth. Treatment attends to relational patterns, internal conflict, defense analysis, and the ways experience may be carried, repeated, and expressed over time.

Nicole earned a Master of Arts in Clinical Counseling and Psychotherapy from the Institute for Clinical Social Work. She is completing a Master of Arts in Psychoanalysis at the Boston Graduate School of Psychoanalysis while continuing psychoanalytic training.

My Approach

Treatment begins through speaking.

Treatment begins through speaking. Free expression allows thoughts, memories, questions, and contradictions to be heard with care. Reflection and introspection can reveal how certain experiences repeat, how meaning is formed, and how conflict is negotiated within the self and in relation to others.

Psychoanalytic and psychodynamic work is not a quick correction of symptoms. It is a disciplined attention to language, repetition, defenses, and the meanings that emerge over time. Through this process, patterns can become more available for thought, and the possibility of change can become more substantial.
